## Email Design Guidelines

**Colors:**
- Primary: #6C4DF4 (LocalLoop purple)
- Gold: #ffd700
- Background: #f8f9fa
- Text: #212529

**Buttons:**
- Primary CTA: Gold background (#ffd700), black text
- Secondary CTA: Purple background (#6C4DF4), white text
- Text links: Purple (#6C4DF4)

**Header:**
LocalLoop logo + "âœ¨ Gold" badge

**Footer:**
- Links: Features | Pricing | Support | Blog
- Social: Twitter | Facebook | Instagram
- Legal: Privacy Policy | Terms of Service | Unsubscribe

**Best Practices:**
- Keep subject lines under 50 characters
- Mobile-first design
- Clear single CTA per email
- Include unsubscribe link
- Personal touch (use first name)
- Show value, not just features
