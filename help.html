Frequently Asked Questions

How do I join my neighborhood?

After creating your account, set your location in the app. LocalLoop will automatically connect you with verified neighbors in your area. You may need to verify your address.

What's the difference between Basic, Premium, and Gold?

Basic: Free plan with 5 posts/day, basic themes, and 5 lifetime AI avatars.
Premium (R49.99/month): Unlimited posts, 10 AI avatars/month, premium themes, ad-free.
Gold (R149.99/month): Everything in Premium + GPT-4o AI features, 20 Vision-enhanced avatars/month, custom prompts.

How do AI Cartoon Avatars work?

Upload a profile photo, choose from 8 art styles (Pixar, Anime, Disney, etc.), and our AI generates a cartoon version. Gold users can also create custom styles with text prompts.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes! You can cancel anytime from your account settings. You'll retain access until the end of your current billing period.

How do I report inappropriate content?

Tap the three dots on any post or comment and select "Report." Our moderation team reviews all reports within 24 hours.

Is my location data shared with others?

No. We only show your approximate neighborhood, never your exact address. Location data is used solely to connect you with nearby neighbors.

How does the marketplace work?

Premium and Gold users can create marketplace listings to sell items or promote services. Transactions happen directly between neighbors.

What languages does LocalLoop support?

The app is available in English. Premium and Gold users get AI-powered translation in 11 South African languages including Zulu, Xhosa, Afrikaans, and more.
