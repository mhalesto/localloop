# Privacy Policy for LocalLoop Connect

**Last Updated:** January 2025

## Introduction

LocalLoop Connect ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Account Information
- Email address (for authentication)
- Username and display name (for public profiles, optional)
- Profile photo (optional)
- Location data (city, province, country - user-selected, not GPS)

### Content You Create
- Posts and comments
- Poll responses
- Status updates
- Direct messages
- Marketplace listings

### Automatically Collected Information
- Device information (type, operating system)
- App usage statistics
- Crash reports and error logs

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Provide and maintain the app's functionality
- Enable you to post content and interact with other users
- Personalize your experience based on your selected location
- Send you notifications about interactions with your content
- Improve app performance and user experience
- Prevent abuse and enforce our terms of service

## Anonymous Posting

When you post anonymously:
- Your identity is not revealed to other users
- Posts are associated with your account internally for moderation purposes
- We may use aggregate, anonymized data for analytics

## Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data only in these circumstances:
- With your explicit consent
- To comply with legal obligations
- To protect our rights and prevent abuse
- With service providers who help us operate the app (e.g., Firebase, cloud storage)

## Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including:
- Encrypted data transmission
- Secure authentication via Firebase
- Regular security audits
- Access controls and monitoring

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Correct inaccurate information
- Delete your account and associated data
- Opt-out of non-essential notifications
- Export your data

## Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 12. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 12. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or via email.

## Data Retention

- Active account data: Retained while your account is active
- Deleted accounts: Data deleted within 30 days of account deletion
- Backups: May be retained for up to 90 days

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:
- **Firebase** (Google) - Authentication, database, and cloud storage
- **Expo** - App development and push notifications

These services have their own privacy policies governing their use of your information.

## Location Data

We collect only the location you manually select (city, province, country). We do not collect precise GPS coordinates or track your physical location unless you explicitly grant permission for specific features.
