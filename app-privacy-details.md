# App Privacy Details for App Store Connect

Use this guide to fill in the App Privacy section in App Store Connect.

## Navigation
App Store Connect → Your App → App Privacy → Get Started

## Data Collection Overview

Answer "Yes" to: **Does your app collect data from this app?**

---

## Data Types to Declare

### 1. Contact Info
**Collect: YES**

#### Email Address
- **Used for**: App Functionality, Developer's Advertising or Marketing
- **Linked to User**: YES
- **Used for Tracking**: NO

---

### 2. User Content
**Collect: YES**

#### Photos or Videos
- **Used for**: App Functionality
- **Linked to User**: YES
- **Used for Tracking**: NO

#### Other User Content (Posts, Comments, Messages)
- **Used for**: App Functionality
- **Linked to User**: YES (for public posts), NO (for anonymous posts)
- **Used for Tracking**: NO

---

### 3. Identifiers
**Collect: YES**

#### User ID
- **Used for**: App Functionality
- **Linked to User**: YES
- **Used for Tracking**: NO

---

### 4. Usage Data
**Collect: YES**

#### Product Interaction
- **Used for**: Analytics, App Functionality
- **Linked to User**: NO
- **Used for Tracking**: NO

#### Crash Data
- **Used for**: App Functionality
- **Linked to User**: NO
- **Used for Tracking**: NO

---

### 5. Location
**Collect: YES**

#### Coarse Location (City/Region)
- **Used for**: App Functionality
- **Linked to User**: YES
- **Used for Tracking**: NO

**Note**: Users manually select their city; no GPS tracking

---

## Data NOT Collected

- ❌ Health & Fitness
- ❌ Financial Info
- ❌ Precise Location
- ❌ Browsing History
- ❌ Search History
- ❌ Contacts
- ❌ Sensitive Info

## Summary

This app collects:
- Email addresses for authentication
- User-generated content (posts, messages, photos)
- Coarse location data (manually selected city)
- Usage and crash data for app improvement

Most data is linked to the user's identity except:
- Anonymous posts
- Aggregated analytics
- Crash reports

No data is used for tracking or third-party advertising.
